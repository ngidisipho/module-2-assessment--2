About us

WELCOME TO PINE CREST SHOPPING CENTRE Pine Crest Centre’s renovations are well underway and the centre is in a state of transformation. Shoppers at Pine Crest will soon see a premier shopping centre emerging with an extra 6000sqm of new retail area. The scope of work includes; improving aesthetics both interior and exterior, upgraded ablution facilities, new and refurbished escalators, new lighting and flooring. Once tiling of the mall is nearing completion, new ceilings and some shop fronts will be installed; On completion of the project, the centre will host new features such as a contemporary and urban food court area including a kiddie’s play area. Vukile is a property holding and investment company, which was listed on the JSE Limited on 24 June 2004 and on the Namibian Stock Exchange on 11 July 2007. Its listing was promoted by Sanlam, which contributed a significant share of Vukile's start-up portfolio. Vukile's market capitalisation was approximately R5.36 billion at 31 March 2012 and its property portfolio was valued at R6.1 billion at year end.
